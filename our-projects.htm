Livs Streaming Service

Online Media Streaming Service

Livs is a streaming media service that provides users to stream voice and video online, it's a useful service to stream conferences, courses or create an internet radio station.

Live streaming, which refers to content delivered live over the Internet, requires a camera for the media, an encoder to digitize the content, a media publisher, and a content delivery network to distribute and deliver the content.
